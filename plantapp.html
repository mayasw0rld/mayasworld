Fall 2024

The Plant App Design

College Project

Collaborating in a team of four, I helped design a mobile and tablet application that bridges high-tech utility with nature. We developed a modern, "tech-meets-nature" aesthetic to provide pet-friendly plant care and instant AI-driven solutions, ensuring a seamless and responsive experience across all devices.

[User Experience Design]
[User Interface Design]
[Prototyping]

The Process

  • Market & User Research: Analyzed competitors and developed detailed personas to identify the specific pain points of urban gardeners and pet owners.
  • Strategic Wireframing: Mapped out user journeys and wireframes to ensure a fast, intuitive path from "problem" to "AI solution."
  • Visual Identity: Established a cohesive digital style guide and UI kit, balancing a nature-inspired palette with modern "tech" iconography.
  • Iterative Design: Refined navigation and layouts to maintain a "simple yet informative" UX that balances information density with clean white space.

User Testing

High-Fidelity Prototyping: Used Figma to create a fully interactive prototype, simulating complex AI interactions and transitions. Data-Driven Iteration: Conducted user testing via Maze, using heatmaps and mission success rates to refine the UI and fix navigation friction points.

The Results

I transformed Citizencentric’s digital presence into a cohesive, high-performance hub. The final platform balances complex content with a polished aesthetic, providing a streamlined experience that the client can now manage independently.
