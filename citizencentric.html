Citizencentric Website

Freelance Project

I transformed Citizencentric’s digital presence by digitizing a 30-page curriculum into a modern, accessible platform designed to handle high-volume content with ease.

[User Interface Design]

The Process

  • Project Management: Managed a 2-month timeline with real-time links to progress and working files.
  • Discovery & Audit: Conducted competitor reviews and site audits to identify key UX improvements.
  • Strategic Planning: Anchored the design with sitemaps and user flows to simplify the 30-page curriculum and develop standardized, cohesive layouts.

The Challenges

  • Information Density: Balanced high-volume data with a clean, engaging interface.
  • Platform Balance: Navigated Squarespace’s constraints by balancing custom code with the client’s need for easy, independent updates.

The Results

I transformed Citizencentric’s digital presence into a cohesive, high-performance hub. The final platform balances complex content with a polished aesthetic, providing a streamlined experience that the client can now manage independently.
