ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Maya. I recently graduated from the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada. Although, my creative journey began much before I started college. I grew up in Downtown Ottawa, always leaning towards making art or playing sports instead of playing with toys. As I got older, I took interest in marketing—but specifically how visuals can attract different people and alter their experience with people and organizations alike.

I offer services including graphic design and branding, web design and development, and photography and illustration. I ensure thorough communication, flexibility and passion.

Have an idea you want to bring to life, reach out to me at maya@mayasworld.ca or fill out the contact form below.
